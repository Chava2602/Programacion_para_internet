Carrot Cake

Ingredients:

  • 3 grated carrots
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the ingredients and preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, cinnamon, salt, and baking powder.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs and add the oil and vanilla extract.
  4. Incorporate the grated carrots into the egg and oil mixture.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, stirring well.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cake to cool before removing it from the pan.

Serving:

Cut the carrot cake into slices and enjoy with a dollop of whipped cream or ice cream.
